How to Play

Four pillars

01

Choose your tier

Easy through Insane. Switch any time — including mid‑round, which surrenders the present game and starts a fresh one.

02

Strike Pedro

Tap him as he appears. He retreats quickly. Successive hits within 1.4 seconds compose a streak — each strike is worth more than the last.

03

Catch Tobi

He flashes briefly and rarely. A successful strike pays fifteen at minimum, up to forty‑five if your streak is hot.

04

Avoid Oliver

He looks the part. Read carefully. A wrong strike costs five points and ends your streak.
