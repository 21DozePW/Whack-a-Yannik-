02 · The Cast

Two gentlemen.
One pays. One does not.

Strike

Yannik

The mark of the round. Each strike awards one point. Successive hits within a brief window compose a streak — each subsequent strike is worth more than the last, up to a multiplier of ×9.

  • +1Per strike
  • ×9Maximum streak multiplier
  • 1.4sTo extend a streak

Avoid

Oliver

Uninvited. He arrives without notice and smiles indistinguishably. A strike upon him is a costly mistake — five points and your streak in the same blow. Discretion is the chief virtue.

  • −5Per misplaced strike
  • ×1Streak resets at once
  • 15–34%Likelihood per appearance

03 · How to Play

Four pillars
of a good round.

01

Choose your tier

Easy through Insane. You may switch at any time — including mid-round, which surrenders the present game and commences a fresh one.

02

Strike Yannik

Tap him as he appears. He retreats quickly. Each successful strike is awarded a point; rapid succession compounds your reward.

03

Avoid Oliver

He bears a passing resemblance. Read the room. A mistaken strike costs you five points and the streak you have so carefully composed.

04

Pursue the ledger

Thirty seconds per round. Your personal best is preserved on this device. The round restarts of its own accord — should you wish, hold the countdown.
